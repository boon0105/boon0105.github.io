First, you had to apply to two companies that you would like to know more information about. The first period was easy for me, Damen is a company which I already knew but didn't know alot about. The second period I visited veracode, this was way to complicated for me and also not that interesting.

In the afternoon we had to make a pster for a company, this company was given to us. At first I wasn't that interested in the company Atos. But when looking up more information about it I started to enjoy it.


Why would I like to work here?

The size of the company is very impressive and because the company is home in so many branches you can basically work in wathever situation you want to.

The company also gives alot of oppotunities to grow and become a better version of yourself, this is very interesting to me.

The only downside that I could think of is that because of the size of the company there might not be alot of personal attention for if you (for exemple) have questinos.

Why would I like to work here?

I've always liked going to the Damen shipyard in Vlissingen when I was younger. But I thought that there wouldn't be a job for me there now that I'm doing the ICT study, so naturally when I heard that there were alot of jobs for us at Damen I was very happy.

I would like to work at Damen because of the size. All the boats they built are different so it's always an adventure what's going to happen.

The only downside that I can think of is that there are alot of secrets that come with the job, and it's difficault for me to keep those to my grandpa.

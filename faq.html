FAQ

Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at hz?

A: Go to http://print.hz.nl and log in with your hz information, then you can upload your file. If you go to the printer and scan your student card you will be able to print the document


Q:How can you scan a document and sent it to your laptop at hz?

A: When you show your HZ pass to the right of the printer downstairs off the hz building you can scan the document that you want to scan. When you've scanned it, it will be send to you inbox.


Q: What do you need to do when are sick / show symptoms of corona virus?

A: Contact my teachers that you wont be coming to school. Then you have to take a test and while you wait for the results you can follow all the lessons at home.


Q: How can you book a project room in one of the wings?

A: If you go to the portal.hz > selfservice portal > make a reservation > reserve a meeting room > pick a date and time and save it.


Q: What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the hz parking lot?

A: Unfornately it's not possible to park your car at the parking lot next to the hz because of the construction on the side. It is possible to park your car for free at the PZEM building
